ENDODONTISTS

Endodontists are sometimes called root canal dentists. While general
dentists and endodontists both perform root canal treatment,
endodontists perform this procedure much more often. The additional
training and higher treatment volume mean that endodontists are the
experts in doing root canals

ORTHODONTISTS

Orthodontists specialize in teeth and jaw alignment,
using wires, braces, retainers, and other devices. If you have an overbite,
underbite, crossbite, or misaligned teeth, you may be referred to an
orthodontist for correction.

COSMETIC DENTISTRY

Cosmetic dentistry is a method of professional oral care that
focuses on improving the appearance of your teeth. And
although cosmetic dentistry procedures are usually elective
rather than essential, some treatment cases also provide
restorative benefits.
